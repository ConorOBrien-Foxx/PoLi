PiLo: A Polyrhythmic Game

How to Play

Using your finger or any key, tap along each time the Judge hits a corner of a shape! Each Perfect (P!) is worth 3 points, each Great (G.) worth 2, and each Okay (K…) worth 1. Not hitting close enough, or forgetting to hit a note, will result in a Miss (∅) for 0 points.
